  1. Coding (NeoPixels flashing red)
    1. Open the Serial window to view the error messages.
    2. Fix the bugs in your code
    3. Press CTL-C to stop running the code.
    4. Press CTL-D to make it auto-run again
  2. Bluefruit (NeoPixels are all turn white)
    1. Press the reset button once and wait for the device to restart.
    2. If step A doesn't resolve the issue, repeat steps 1-3 and 5-7 from the "Set up your Adafruit board" tab in Initial Steps section of the instructions.
  3. Display

    Make sure all the standoffs on the display are flush with the pads on the circuit board. See step 4 from the "Assembly" tab in Initial Steps section of the instructions.

  4. Servo
    1. Make sure your jumper wires connecting the header to the bread board connect the right header pins to the corresponding servo pins. See step 4 from the "Initial Setup" tab in Robots section of the instructions.
    2. Make sure all your header pins are soldered correctly. See step 2 from the "Assembly" tab in Initial Steps section of the instructions.
