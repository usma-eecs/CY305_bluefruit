  2. This program is a memory game. The display will show "A" or "B", then wait for you to press Button A or B, respectively. Next, the display will show a second "A" or "B", and you will need to enter the new sequence of As and/or Bs. This process continues until you either enter the wrong sequence (you lose) or reach a sequence that is 10 A's and B's long (you win). Try modifying the max_length variable in Line 79 to allow for a longer winning sequence length. Also, try modifying lines 71 and 73 if you want the sequence of letters to display faster for you to memorize.
